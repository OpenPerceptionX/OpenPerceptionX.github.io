Track 1: OpenLane Topology



In OpenLane-V2, we annotate lane centerlines and their connectivities to provide map-like results to facilitate downstream tasks. Moreover, traffic elements and their correspondences to lanes are critical for understanding the complex driving environment. Participants are required to deliver results on lanes, traffic elements, and topology relationships simultaneously.

Track 2: Occupancy Prediction



Unlike previous perception representations, which depend on predefined geometric primitives or perceived data modalities, occupancy enjoys the flexibility to describe entities in arbitrary shapes. Based on the given images, participants are needed to provide the occupancy state and semantics of each voxel in 3D space for the complete scene.

Track 3: nuPlan Planning



Previous benchmarks focus on short-term motion forecasting and are limited to open-loop evaluation. NuPlan introduces long-term planning of the ego vehicle and corresponding metrics. Provided as docker containers, submissions are deployed for simulation and evaluation.
